# Business Analytics Case Study
### Sales, Profitability & Regional Performance Analysis using Python

---

## Case Study Overview

This repository presents an end-to-end Business Analytics Case Study focused on evaluating sales performance, profitability, regional trends, and business growth opportunities using Python.

The project demonstrates a structured analytics workflow—from data preparation and exploratory analysis to generating actionable business insights and strategic recommendations. Rather than simply reporting metrics, the analysis focuses on identifying business problems and supporting data-driven decision-making.

This case study is based on a real-world business analytics assessment and has been developed as a portfolio project to showcase practical analytical and business problem-solving skills.

---

# Business Objectives

The primary objectives of this case study were to:

- Analyze category-wise sales and profitability.
- Evaluate monthly sales target trends.
- Identify high-performing and underperforming regions.
- Assess product experience based on business requirements.
- Generate actionable business recommendations using data.

---

# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The analysis focuses on the following business KPIs:

| KPI | Business Purpose |
|------|------------------|
| **Total Sales** | Measures overall revenue generated across product categories. |
| **Total Profit** | Evaluates profitability generated from sales. |
| **Profit Margin (%)** | Measures operational efficiency and profitability. |
| **Total Orders** | Indicates customer demand and sales volume. |
| **Average Profit per Order** | Evaluates profitability at the transaction level. |
| **Monthly Sales Target** | Assesses sales planning consistency across categories. |

# Key Business Insights

## Sales & Profitability

- Clothing achieved the highest profit margin, indicating strong profitability.
- Electronics generated significant sales but comparatively lower profit margins.
- Furniture recorded the lowest profitability, highlighting opportunities for pricing and operational improvements.

---

## Sales Target Analysis

- Monthly Furniture sales targets showed noticeable fluctuations.
- Historical business performance can be leveraged to establish more consistent and realistic targets.

---

## Regional Performance

- Madhya Pradesh recorded the highest order volume.
- Maharashtra consistently demonstrated strong sales performance.
- Punjab reported negative average profit despite generating sales.
- Gujarat exhibited comparatively lower profitability than other major regions.

---

## Product Experience Assessment

### Strengths

- Simple and intuitive user experience
- Low barrier to begin saving
- Easy onboarding process
- Accessible digital savings model

### Opportunities for Improvement

- Improve withdrawal experience
- Increase pricing transparency
- Enhance customer support responsiveness
- Introduce additional engagement features

# Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations were proposed:

- Improve profitability strategies for Furniture products.
- Replicate successful regional strategies in underperforming markets.
- Utilize historical business data for improved sales target planning.
- Prioritize profitability alongside revenue growth.
- Enhance customer experience by improving critical user journeys and operational transparency.
